What does “add to cart” mean?

Adding a medication to the card is the first step toward requesting a refill or a new prescription. The next step is to provide the necessary information and confirm your order. Then we will process your refill or contact your doctor on your behalf to request a new prescription. Your doctor must approve your prescription requests before they can be filled.
